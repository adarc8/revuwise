RevuWise Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RevuWise ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Extension on a supported hotel page (e.g., Booking.com):

  • Hotel Information: We identify the hotel name and country code from the page URL to determine the hotel being viewed.
  • Review Data: We analyze publicly available reviews displayed on the current hotel page.
  • User Questions: We collect the questions you enter into the chat interface.

This information is transmitted to our backend server for processing.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Process hotel reviews to generate summaries and support semantic search features.
  • Generate answers to your questions based on the processed review data using AI models (e.g., OpenAI).
  • Temporarily store processed review data to enhance performance for repeated queries.
